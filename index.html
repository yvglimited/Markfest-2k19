About Markfest

The two day Marketing Carnival will see a high energy and vibrancy in the campus where the students from various B-schools will unleash their creative talents to emerge as marketing wizards and strategists.

There are 7 events having different flavours of marketing – Digital Advertising, Branding, Selling etc. As your B-school has a prominent place in NCR shaping the young brains towards right vision, may I take the opportunity to invite and request you to nominate a large number of students for various events to participate and win attractive set of cash prizes/medals/trophies that would add to their confidence and knowledge.

The Festive mood will be highlighted with the presence Business Haat, Food Court and the other attractions along with LIVE RADIO, to maintain the effervescence of the bright youth.
